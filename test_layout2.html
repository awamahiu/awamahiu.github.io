  1. The Menu

    The 'Menu Button' is located on the TOP RIGHT section of the ATLAS viewport. To access The Menu, use your mouse to click on it.

    Through the Menu, you can access several powerful Atlas features such as FEEDS, LAYERS, BASE MAPS, and MESSAGES. Other features include HELP, FEEDBACK, SETTINGS, and SIGN OUT.
