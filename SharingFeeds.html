Sharing Feeds

  1. Sending an Invitation
    With Atlas, you are allowed to share feeds that belong to you with anyone who own a valid email address. You have complete control over the feed. From the comfort of your screen, you can update and revoke access to, and delete your shared feeds.

    You can only share feeds that belong to you. Feeds that belong to you are distinguishable by the number of options you have access to under the Feeds Tab.

  2. The Share Manager

    Pins are the visual representaion of the data in a FEED displayed on the Map. The map pins are also clickable. Click a pin to display the information available for that pin.
