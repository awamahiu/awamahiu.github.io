Displaying Feeds

To display a feed, click on the Menu Button and then click on the tab labeled 'FEEDS'.

Under the FEEDS panel, you can interact with your feeds in several ways including ACTIVATION, SHARING, DELETING, and SOMETHING ELSE? Activating a feed makes Pins appear on the Map.
