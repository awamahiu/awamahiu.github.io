Data Feeds

  1. Displaying Feeds
    To display a feed, click on the Menu Button and then click on the tab labeled 'FEEDS'.

    Under the FEEDS panel, you can interact with your feeds in several ways including ACTIVATION, SHARING, DELETING, and SOMETHING ELSE? Activating a feed makes Pins appear on the Map.

    Accessing the Feeds Tab
    Activating a Feed
  2. Pins

    Pins are the visual representaion of the data in a FEED displayed on the Map. The map pins are also clickable. Click a pin to display the information available for that pin.

    Orange and Blue Pins on the map
    Displaying data in a Pin
  3. Legends

    The 'Legend Buttons' appear at the BOTTOM LEFT corner of the viewport whenever a Feed is made visible. Legends stack for each open feed.
