Marketing

Design Brief: Minimalist / Single Page. Design should be simple and easy to navigate. Main call-out at the moment is getting users to sign up.

Notes: V1 & V2 are Single Page designs built with Foundation. V1 has an image slider as the hero and V2 has a Sign Up form. 'Minimalist', built on a Bootstrap template, has a FullScreen / No-Scroll design. Users click on buttons to reveal modals which allow them to carry out whatever task they came to the site for.
Issues:
  • All: Antialiasing of Lato font on iPad (Safari) doesn't work. Seems to be a recurring problem with the typeface. Change font or add weight to current font.
  • Minimalist: Modals do not recall if background is clicked above modal position. On an iPad (Safari), recalling modal by clicking background causes keyboard to slide up.
