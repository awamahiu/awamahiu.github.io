Getting Started

So here's where we are... You've registered and logged into Atlas View! But what next? The first step is familiarizing yourself with where all the important elements are so you can unleash the full power of Atlas.

  1. The Menu Button
    The 'Menu Button' is located on the TOP RIGHT section of the ATLAS viewport. To access The Menu, use your mouse to click on it.

    Through the Menu, you can access several powerful Atlas features such as FEEDS, LAYERS, BASE MAPS, and MESSAGES. Other features include HELP, FEEDBACK, SETTINGS, and SIGN OUT.

    The Menu Button
  2. What is a Feed

    A Feed is the visual representation of CSV data. You can gain access to a 'Feed' either by adding it to ATLAS on your own or by being invited to someone else's feed.

    Click here to learn more about feeds.

    Accessing the Feeds Tab
    Activating a Feed
  3. What is A Layer

    Atlas includes layers, such as Nexrad weather radar, spaghetti models, Florida counties, etc. that can be added to the map. To add layers to the map, click the Atlas MENU, and then click LAYERs. Toggle the VISIBLE switch on to display layers.

  4. What Is A Base Map

    You can change the look and feel of the map, by toggling on different base maps, including:

    Light
    Dark
    Terrain
    Satellite
